Electronic magazines , also known as e-magazines, offer a range of benefits that make them a popular choice in today's digital age.
Interactivity: E-magazines are more interactive than printed versions, providing a better user experience. They can include engaging images, audio, video, 360-degree views, pop-ups, and weblinks, which can enhance user engagement
Cost-Efficiency: Digital magazines are more cost-effective compared to printed magazines. There are no additional expenses on printing, binding, and delivering, and no losses occur due to unsold copies

Analytics: Digital analytics services help authors gain an in-depth understanding of user behavior and usage statistics. This level of usage analytics is not feasible for traditional printed magazines
Sharing: Readers can share content from digital magazines, helping to build the audience
Accessibility:Digital magazines can be accessed anytime and anywhere, providing convenience to the readers
Global Distribution:Digital magazines can be distributed globally, eliminating geographical limitations. They can be accessed via email or push notification, making them available to a global audience
